CONNECTK

ConnectK is an enhanced version of Connect4 (the board game). ConnectK's width (w), height (h), and K are all configurable. ConnectK is also more interesting as it support 2 different gravity modes: ** ON | OFF **. With gravity off, the pieces stay wherever they are placed. With gravity on, they fall till they meet another piece or hit the bottom.
